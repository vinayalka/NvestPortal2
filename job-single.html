Discription

We are an ecommerce solutions provider and have an immediate opening for a Front-End Web Developer. We are a young entrepreneurial company that has established itself as a leader in our market segment for providing exclusive web services.

The ideal candidate is an individual who is interested in a compelling career in web development. While no professional programming experience is required, you will be expected to know basic amounts of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Knowledge of JavaServer pages, and JavaScript libraries like jQuery is a plus. This position is perfect for a recent college graduate. This is a great opportunity to work on enterprise-level projects with high visibility. A dream job for anyone looking to have a career in a stable company with start-up culture and perks.
